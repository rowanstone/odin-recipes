Strawberry Banana Nice Cream

Description

This low fat raw vegan soft-serve style ice cream is remarkably easy to make from home and a wonderful treat to share with the family.

Serves 2 | Total Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 very ripe bananas, peeled and frozen solid, cut in 2 inch pieces
  • Pinch of ground cinnamon
  • Seeds scraped from 1/2 vanilla bean.
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, greens removed

Equipmemt

  • Sharp chopping knife
  • Cutting board
  • High speed blender
  • Rubber spatula
  • Medium mixing bowl
  • 2 bowls for serving

Steps

  1. Put the bananas, cinnamon, and vanilla seeds in the blender and blend until smooth, starting on the lowest speed and working up to the fastest. Blend on the highest speed until the mixture is uniformly smooth. Use the tamper as needed to ensure that everything blends well. Reduce speed back down to the lowest setting before turning it off.
  2. Scrape the banana mixture into the mixing bowl with a spatula.
  3. Slice the strawberries and add them to the mixing bowl to mix together with the banana mixture. Serve the ice cream in the bowls to enjoy immediately!

It is best to eat the banana nice cream immediately because it does not freeze well after melting.
