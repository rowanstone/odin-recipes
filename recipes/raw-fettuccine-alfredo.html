Raw Vegan Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

A healthy and cruelty-free alternative to a classic favorite! This low fat recipe also takes less time to prepare than regular Fettuccine Alfredo.

Serves 2 | Total Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 5 large zucchini, stems removed, peeled if desired
  • 1 celery stalk, cut in chunks
  • 1/4 cup raw pine nuts
  • 1 garlic clove, halved
  • Juice from 1/2 lemon
  • Leaves from 3 fresh basil sprigs
  • 4 fresh sage leaves
  • Florets from 1/2 head of cauliflower, coarsely chopped

Equipmemt

  • Sharp chopping knife
  • Cutting board
  • Citrus juicer
  • Spiral slicer
  • High speed blender
  • Food processor
  • Rubber spatula
  • Large serving bowl

Steps

  1. Use the spiral slicer to make zucchini noodles with 4 of the zucchini. Transfer to the large bowl and set it aside.
  2. Cut the remaining zucchini into chunks and put it in the blender along with the celery, pine nuts, garlic, lemon juice, basil, and sage. Blend until smooth, starting with the lowest speed and gradually working up to the highest. Blend on high speed until the mixture is uniform. Reduce blender speed back down to lowest setting before shutting it off.
  3. Pour the sauce over the noodles and top with garnish if desired. Enjoy immediately!

This recipe is sourced from The Fully Raw Diet book, written by Kristina Carrillo-Bucaram
